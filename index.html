About us

Megannety Consulting provides custom geographic data solutions designed to help real estate professionals, brokerages, and local businesses operate more effectively within their markets. From hyper-local lead mapping to advanced spatial analysis, the focus is on delivering actionable insights through the power of data. Each solution is tailored to support smarter decision-making and more efficient fieldwork.

Services Offered

  • Lead Intelligence Reports: Downloadable data sheets with phone, email, and addresses
  • Territory Optimization: Efficient zone creation for agent or team coverage
  • FSBO & Expired Listings (Optional): Integration into your personalized datasets
  • White-label Solutions: For teams or brokerages looking to scale their own branded tools
